Core Values Reinforcement

Celebrate Employees
Who Live Your Values

Celebrate Employees
Who Live Your Values

Powered by Fond RewardsTM, our Core Values Program offers senior leadership and managers a simple and direct way of recognizing employees when they uphold company values. Any time an employee acts in alignment with corporate values, managers can send reward credits along with a personal congratulatory message that can be shared company-wide. Employees can choose from hundreds of high-quality rewards spanning diverse categories, offering something for everyone.
